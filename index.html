Dice Force
- Prague Club -

We are a community whos passionate of TRPG (tabletop role-playing game) in the city of Prague. We meet to talck about game and do some event together. We also help new personne who come at Prague to form a party of player and give advise and support for some who whant to become GM (Game Master) to establish a Campagne/Quest.
Folow our Facebook and join our activity/discussion.

Atelier

TheoryCraft : Once per month we meet for discuss/debat about the rules of the game or about the univers rule we can have in TRPG for devlop and goes deeper inside the game. In can be about character creation, politics of différente faction, the différent environement...ect
The cible it's more for GM but we welcome everybody who are interested about the création of univers.

The Role-play Nigth : Once a year we organise an amazing event who regroupe everybody of the club on the form of a giant Role play Game. We desgise ourself to match the theme of the night it can be Pirate, Space opera, Medieval, Fantaisie... and more and more! It's an incontournable of the club and an amazing event to participate.
Information for participate send by email or in the Facebook of the club and menber must book thier participation.
